Frequent Flyer Program

Frequent Flyers can redeem VIP cards!



Sliver card:
The consumer can get a short trip ticket for free after finishing 18000 miles trip, silver consumers can enjoy the medium priority channel in the airport. ( Conditions to be met: consumers need to have their own accounts and pay for 15 dollars every year)


Golden Card:
Enjoy all silver card’s goods. Consumers have unlimited laggages. (Conditions to be met: silver consumers who finish 200000 miles trip and pay for 20 dollars every year)


Demon Card:
Enjoy all golden card’s goods. Consumers may have chances to upgrade their tickets from tourist to the business class. Consumers can use the highest priority channel in the airport. (Conditions to be met: golden members who finish 880000 miles trip and pay for 30 dollars every year)


Black Card:
Enjoy all demon members’ goods. Consumers have their own customer service which is 24 hours online. They have the right to chartering planes and they will be able to have a free Champagne every time they book a flight. (Condition to be met: demon members who finish 5000000 miles and pay 2000 every year for management fee)
