Transportation condition




We have several conditions for your baggages for your and other passanger's safety:
The maximum weight of each piece of checked baggage for US flights on domestic / international flights that are related to our company's international or regional flights must not exceed 45 kg (90 pounds), and the sum of each three sides must not exceed 158 cm (62 inches); For non-US routes, the maximum weight of each piece of checked baggage must not exceed 32 kg (70 lbs), and the sum of the three sides of each piece must not exceed 158 cm (62 inches).


Items not to be carried as baggage:
1. Dangerous goods including:
      1) explosives;
      2) gases, including flammable gases, non-flammable, non-toxic gases, and toxic gases;
      3) flammable liquids;
      4) flammable solids, spontaneous combustible substances, and easily released in contact with water Fuel gas substances;
      5) oxidants and organic peroxides;
      6) toxic and infectious substances;
      7) radioactive substances
      8) corrosive substances;
      9) miscellaneous dangerous goods such as magnetic substances.

2. Other dangerous goods that are not allowed to be carried as luggage by China Southern Airlines.

3. Narcotic, unpleasant or other substances with similar odor or properties.

4. Items that easily deface the aircraft.

5, firearms, including various simulation toy guns, gun-type lighters and other various types of offensive weapons, except for sports firearms.

6. Ordnance and police equipment.

7. control tools.

8. Live animals, except small animals and service dogs that meet the requirements.

9. Other prohibited items stipulated by the state.
